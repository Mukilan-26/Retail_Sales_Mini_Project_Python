# Retail Sales Data Analysis – Mini Project  

## Overview  
This project analyzes retail sales data (3,000 records) using Python and Pandas to uncover business insights related to sales performance, profitability, seasonal trends, and customer behavior. The dataset includes product categories, regional sales, monthly transactions, and customer purchase details.  

The objective is to help retail businesses make data-driven decisions that improve profitability, optimize inventory planning, and strengthen customer engagement.  

---

## Business Objectives  
- **Sales Distribution**: Identify which product categories contribute most to overall profit and sales.  
- **Regional Performance**: Evaluate which region performs best in terms of sales and profit.  
- **Profitability Insights**: Assess whether higher sales volumes consistently lead to higher profits.  
- **Seasonal Trends**: Detect months with significant spikes or dips in sales.  
- **Customer Behavior**: Analyze frequent customers and their contribution to total revenue.  

---

## Key Insights  

### Category-wise Profit  
| Category        | Profit (₹)     |  
|-----------------|----------------|  
| Furniture       | 404,251.04     |  
| Office Supplies | 362,634.69     |  
| Technology      | 349,755.87     |  

**Conclusion**: Furniture is the most profitable category, with Chairs being the top-selling subcategory.  

---

### Regional Performance

| South  | 1,838,119       | 279,152.36     |  
| West   | 1,854,004       | 279,236.59     |  

**Conclusion**: The North region leads in both sales and profit, followed by West and South.
